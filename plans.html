Service Plans

At Ormond Lawn Care, we take care to provide our customers high quality services personalized for their unique needs. We are available to handle your needs in a consistent and timely manner. There is no job to large or small for Ormond Lawn Care.

All plans include 50 service visits per year, with an Ormond Lawn Care Foreman on your property at all times during our service visit. Our uniformed staff will complete their duties promptly and efficiently, in order to disturb you as little as possible.

Please Note: The size and labor intensity of each customers property can vary dramatically, from small one room residential, to large commercial properties. Please Request a Quote by calling or filling out the form on this webpage.

Basic Plan

Includes lawn mowing, edging of curbs, walkways, and paved areas, strong line trimming around obstacles, and blowing of all debris.

Plus Plan

Includes all Basic Plan services plus mulching/bagging of seasonal leaves, trimming of hedges under 8', and weed control.

Premium Plan

Includes all Basic Plan services plus installation and supply of Red Mulch in Spring, quarterly fertilization and insecticide, and yearly palm tree trimming.
